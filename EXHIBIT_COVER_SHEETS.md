# EXHIBIT COVER SHEETS
**FDSJ-739-2024 | Pre-Formatted Templates**

## Purpose

These cover sheets should be attached to the front of each physical exhibit when filing with the court. They provide:
- Clear exhibit identification
- Description of what the exhibit proves
- Date and source information
- Legal relevance

---

## How to Use

1. **Copy the template for each exhibit** (Ctrl+C, Ctrl+V)
2. **Fill in the bracketed information** [like this]
3. **Print on standard 8.5"x11" paper**
4. **Attach to front of exhibit** (paper clip or staple to top-left)
5. **Insert into appropriate binder tab**

## Printing Instructions

2. **Attach to FRONT of exhibit** (paper clip or staple top-left corner)
3. **Organize by tab** (see Filing Strategy for 7-tab binder structure)
